Why
ISNAD ?

We specialize in supporting university students in Gaza by providing comprehensive support that combines material and knowledge-based resources to create sustainable change.

  • We think outside the box – seeking innovative solutions in light of the challenges students face.
  • Quality in everything we do – we are committed to high standards in selecting beneficiaries and providing support.
  • Your success is our success – we achieve our goals when your dreams come true.

A place where ideas are born and ambitions grow. An environment designed to inspire university students and freelancers to shape their future with creativity.

What We Do

We offer free, fully equipped workspaces with all the technology and facilities you need, specialized training programs to develop your skills, and moral and social support for students. Contribute to a better future for the students of Gaza.
